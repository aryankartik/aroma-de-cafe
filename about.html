Our Story

Bringing the heart and soul of Colombia to London.

Established 2015

A Family Tradition

Founded in 2015, Aroma de Café began with a simple dream: to share the authentic flavors of Colombian home cooking with the diverse community of London.

We are a family-run business that believes food is the language of love. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, ensuring that every Arepa, Empanada, and Sancocho tastes just like it does back home in Colombia.

We pride ourselves on using fresh ingredients and traditional cooking methods to create an atmosphere that is warm, inviting, and unmistakably Latin.

Our Mission

"To provide an authentic culinary experience that transports our guests to the streets of Bogotá and Medellín, serving high-quality food with the warmth of Colombian hospitality."
